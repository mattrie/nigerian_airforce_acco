ABOUT US
What we represent?
At The PRIDE, we stand with the valiant officers and men of the Nigerian Armed Forces who continuously make sacrifices of varying degrees to ensure the safety, security, and integrity of our beloved nation. These selfless individuals endure discomfort and life-threatening situations in their unwavering service to our fatherland.
The PRIDE has taken up this mantle, leveraging a social enterprise model to focus on the paramount goal of enhancing the welfare of our military personnel. Our approach also includes generating funds to ensure the lasting success of our initiatives. Sustainability has been a challenge for previous welfare-focused efforts, and we are committed to reliably achieving our organization's goals.

What We Offer
Elevate Your Lifestyle with Exclusive Savings and Military Discounts.
Health Insurance Support
Beyond military health insurance, officers, men, and their dependents can benefit from private health insurance packages at a 50% discount, ensuring access to top-tier private hospitals.
Hospitality Support
Hospitality organizations such as partner hotels shall offer discounted room rates for officers and men of the armed forces who may be stranded or who require such on transit
Shop Support
The PRIDE Shop shall be opened to the officers and men of the military for those who wish to buy daily household commodities, utilities and equipment at discounted rates of up to 50%.
Finance Support
Financial institutions, encompassing banks, microfinance banks, and others, dedicated to providing (i) expedited services for all armed forces officers and personnel and (ii) offering reduced interest rates on loans to support officers and personnel of the armed forces.
